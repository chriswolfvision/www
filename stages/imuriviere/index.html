Post-doc position in computer vision open at the LIRIS laboratory (CNRS)

Learning based object recognition from HD videos

Subject

We offer a post-doc position in computer science in the LIRIS laboratory located in Lyon, France. The research subject will be related to computer vision and machine learning, especially learning-based object detection and recognition in videos. A particular focus will be deep learning and a combination with regularization.

The targeted application concerns the automated analysis of first person-view image sequences acquired using a mobile camera (glasses) endowed with an eyetracking system, the goal being detection and recognition of various focused scene elements.

The post-doc will be funded by the French RIVIERE project, which is part of LabEx IMU. The project is a joint effort of several laboratories located in Lyon, conducting research in various fields, namely computer science, geography and ecology.

Duration/Starting date: The position is currently open for 12 months, starting in january 2014 if possible.
